Card adjusted data up to Mar 31 2020, Web scraping data up to Mar 31 2020

Key Takeaways
  • We estimate TAL Net Revenue to grow at +14%-19% y/y (in USD) in F4Q20 (ending Feb-20), slowed down from +47% y/y in F3Q20, in-line with preliminary result of +17-20% y/y and  consensus +19% y/y; We estimate Xueersi Peiyou Small Class to grow at +11%-16% y/y (in USD) in F4Q20, slowed down from +38% y/y in F3Q20.
  • In March, web scraping data captured TAL net revenue to grow +39% y/y in RMB), slightly accelerated from +29% y/y in F4Q20, likely benefited by online course transition, while card adjusted data showed offline order intake remained sluggish.
Sandalwood Card Data Suggest Offline Order Intake sluggish due to Corona Virus
  • Sandalwood card data consistently captured ~30%-40% of company reported order intake revenue, mostly offline class revenues. 
  • Due to Covid-19 at the end of Jan 2020, we observed offline order intake almost become zero and some refund happened post CNY.
  • Our card data showed F4Q20 growth at -87% y/y due to large refund in Feb. The offline weakness continued to March with no order intake.

  • Sandalwood Web Scraping is Relevant and Predictive
  • Sandalwood web scraping data tracked revenue from both Xueersi Peiyou website (www.speiyou.com) and Xueersi Peiyou mobile app, and captured ~70-80% of company reported total net revenue and almost all of reported Xueersi Peiyou small class revenue. As of Nov-19, our web scraping data captured ~80% of company reported total number of learning centers. This make the data very relevant.
  • Sandalwood historical data has decent correlation with company reported net revenue and Peiyou small class revenue, indicating good prediction power.

Sandalwood Data Suggests TAL Revenue to slow down in F4Q20
  • Sandalwood web scraping data grow at +29% y/y in F4Q20, with +66% y/y in Feb thanks to 1) easy comps 2) shift from offline to online courses,  slowed down from +46% y/y in F3Q20. 
    • Our web scraped data showed overall revenue softened mainly driven by most of Top 5 cities which accounted for ~55% of total revenue.
    • For rest of cities, company reported growth rate at +42% y/y in USD, ~+43% y/y in RMB, close to our web scraped data at +39%y/y in RMB in the Nov -19 ending quarter.

Assumption and Data Caveat
  • Company adopted new accounting standard since Mar 2018, while prior period deferred revenue are not adjusted, which made calculated order intake value in F1Q19 (ending May-18) smaller than if it were adjusted.
